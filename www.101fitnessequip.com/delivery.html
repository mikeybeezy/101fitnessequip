 Royal Mail Shipping:
 101fitnessequip will deliver your order via Royal Mail delivery service.
  
 Royal Mail Delivery:
 Royal Mail delivers between 8 AM and 6 PM on business days. Our business days are Monday through Friday, except for generally observed public holidays. Unfortunately Royal Mail does not deliver to PO boxes. All deliveries must be signed for upon receipt. Upon delivery, please inspect the packaging for damage. If it appears that the products are damaged, please do not accept the shipment. If you find that there is a problem with your order/product after receipt of the order/product, please refer to the Returns/Refund page. Once delivery is completed, you bear the risk for the items. We will try our upmost to deliver your products, but we reserve the right to cancel your order in the event that no one is available to sign for your shipment.
  
 What happens if I'm not at home when my delivery comes?
 Royal will try at least twice to deliver your order to the address indicated by you. In case Royal Mail cannot deliver at the indicated address, the driver will leave an Information Notice in the mailbox. After two delivery attempts, Royal Mail will keep your package for a maximum of 7 business days. After 7 business days, Royal Mail will return your package back to 101fitnessequip. If you wish to change the delivery details, please follow the procedure explained on the Information Notice.
  
 Email with Shipment Notification
 You will receive a shipment notification email from 101fitnessequip when your products have shipped from our fulfillment center. This shipment notification will hold the Royal Mail track and trace number of your order. From the email you can link to the Royal Mail track and trace site.
